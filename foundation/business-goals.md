# Fooladno — Business Goals
## Phase 1 · Foundation — #2

**Version:** 1.0 · 26 June 2026
**Companion:** `docs/vision.md` (the why), `docs/market-research.md` (the evidence).
**Purpose:** What success looks like for the *business* — objectives, model, market, positioning, economics, and milestones.

---

## 1. Strategic Objectives
1. **Trust leadership.** Become the most *trusted, modern* place to buy iron & steel in Iran (brand + transparency + AI).
2. **A qualified-lead engine.** Convert anonymous search/price-checking traffic into **warm, context-rich leads** for the sales desk — at low acquisition cost (organic/SEO + Telegram + AI).
3. **Higher conversion & margin.** Use trust, the AI advisor, and guaranteed delivery time to win the order and protect margin against price-only competitors.
4. **A retained audience.** Turn one-time price-checkers into returning users (alerts, club, content) → cheaper repeat sales.
5. **Platform expansion.** Web → web app → mobile app (Cafe Bazaar/Myket), then new revenue lines (credit, marketplace, data).

## 2. Business Model
**Phase 1 (now): lead-gen + direct/aggregated sale.** Fooladno curates supply and prices, the website generates qualified demand, and the **sales team closes by phone** (no online payment yet). Revenue = **trading margin** on steel sold; the platform's job is to *lower CAC, raise conversion, and protect margin* versus the cluttered price-list incumbents.

**Future revenue lines (Roadmap):**
- **Embedded credit margin** (چک/LC/installments → BNPL) — the India lesson (OfBusiness/Oxyzo).
- **Marketplace commissions** (verified third-party suppliers — «تأمین از شما» → marketplace).
- **Subscription / data** (a trusted price index «شاخص فولادنو», alerts, API — the Mysteel/BigMint lesson).
- **Club & advertising** (premium tiers; mill/brand placements).
- **Logistics margin** (integrated freight) — later.

## 3. Market Opportunity
- **Large, essential, FX-linked commodity** with constant demand (construction, manufacturing, infrastructure) and **daily-volatile prices**.
- **Fragmented & digitally underserved:** hundreds of look-alike SEO price sites, phone-only sales, almost **no trusted two-sided marketplace, no real online conversion, opaque bourse→بنگاه pricing** (see `docs/market-research.md`).
- **Structural conditions resemble China/India pre-platform** (where the model *worked*) far more than the consolidated West (where neutral marketplaces *failed*). That's the opening.

## 4. Competitive Positioning
- **Vs Iranian incumbents** (red, cluttered, phone-only): we are **calm, transparent, AI-guided, conversion-ready** — premium where they are busy.
- **Vs failed Western "Amazon-for-steel"** (Reibus/Felux/XOM): we **don't bet on a thin neutral marketplace**; we lead with **trust + advice + a real sell-side relationship + credit/logistics later**.
- **The wedge nobody owns:** a trusted AI advisor + radical price transparency + **guaranteed delivery time** + modern-but-traditional sales.

## 5. Value Proposition
- **For buyers:** decide with confidence (AI), trust the price (transparent + delivery time), buy the way they already do (human close, no payment friction).
- **For the business:** cheaper acquisition (SEO/AI), higher conversion (trust + context-armed sales), stickier customers (alerts/club), and a path to higher-margin lines (credit/data/marketplace).

## 6. Unit-Economics Drivers (what we optimize)
| Lever | Driver |
|---|---|
| **CAC** | organic SEO share, Telegram/Eitaa channels, AI as free top-of-funnel |
| **Lead→Won %** | warm context for sales, trust signals, delivery-time, credit options |
| **AOV / margin** | bundle (cart/BOM), advisory up-sell, factory mix |
| **Repeat rate / LTV** | alerts, club tiers, content, reorder |
| **Ops efficiency** | fast admin price entry, automated پیش‌فاکتور, CRM |

## 7. Business KPIs (targets set with the founder; see `success-metrics.md`)
- Monthly **qualified leads** and **lead→won %**; **closed orders/GMV**; **AOV & margin/ton**.
- **Repeat-purchase rate**; **club members**; **alert subscribers**.
- **Organic traffic share** and **price-page rankings**.
- **Ops:** % SKUs fresh daily; lead response time.

## 8. Risks & Mitigations
| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **FX / price volatility** | intraday admin updates, quote-validity lock, transparency |
| **Daily price-entry burden** | fast admin grid + stale-flagging + bulk import |
| **AI accuracy/trust** | grounding (tools), validation, callback fallback |
| **AI reachability (sanctions)** | server-side DeepSeek via out-of-Iran relay + fallback |
| **Trust takes time** | eNamad/اتحادیه, mill/customer logos, guarantee, content/SEO |
| **Competition copies us** | own the AI + data + delivery-time brand + credit moat |

## 9. Business Milestones (phased)
1. **Launch (MVP):** trusted price site + AI + lead engine live; first qualified leads → first sales.
2. **Traction (v1.1):** alerts/charts/channels; rising organic share; repeat sales.
3. **Stickiness (v1.2):** club + content engine; measurable retention.
4. **Scale (Phase 2/3):** web app + mobile app; expand catalog/regions.
5. **New margin (Future):** credit, marketplace, data/index, logistics.
