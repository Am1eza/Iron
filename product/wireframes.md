# Fooladno — Wireframes
## Layer 2 · Product Design — Document 8 of N

**Version:** 1.0 · 26 June 2026
**Status:** Draft for approval
**Builds on:** `ux-flow.md`, `user-stories.md`, `acceptance-criteria.md`
**Fidelity:** Low-fidelity **structural** wireframes (layout + content + states). Brand styling (color/type/spacing per `brand-book.md`) is applied in the later UI-design step.

### Conventions
- ASCII frames are **schematic**. The real product is **RTL**: the **category rail sits on the RIGHT**, content reads right-to-left, primary actions sit right. Region labels make placement explicit.
- `[ ]` button · `(•)` toggle/radio · `____` input · `▾` dropdown · `«»` Persian label · `▦` image/logo · `↑↓` نوسان arrows · `◎` state.
- Every data region declares states: **loading / empty / error / stale**.
- **Breakpoints:** Desktop ≥1024 · Tablet 768–1023 · Mobile ≤767 (primary).
- **Maps to:** US-/AC-/F- references.

### Screen index
Global frame · 1 Home(desktop) · 2 Home(mobile) · 3 Category/Price-Table(desktop) · 4 Price-Table(mobile) · 5 SKU detail+chart · 6 AI conversation · 7 طلا و ارز · 8 Inquiry cart · 9 Request→OTP→پیش‌فاکتور→confirm · 10 وزن‌سنج · 11 پروژه‌سنج · 12 Alert modal · 13 Account · 14 Club popup+page · 15 News/Blog · 16 Why-us/About/Contact · 17 Cooperation · 18 Admin price grid · 19 Admin CRM · 20 Admin content · 21 Admin catalog · 22 Admin dashboard.
