## 01 · Brand Strategy

### The name
**آهن‌تایم / Ahantime.** In Persian, **آهن‌تایم** is a real adjective — *"steely, made of steel."* It carries strength, resilience, temper, and permanence, without being a literal "آهن/فولاد + X" label like every competitor. In Latin, **"Ahantime"** reads as a confident, modern, almost international brand — perfect for the app stores and future growth. One word, premium, ownable, and instantly on-category.

### Brand essence
**همراه هوشمند، خرید مطمئن** — *A smart companion, a confident purchase.*

### Positioning statement
For Iranian builders, contractors, and buyers who find the iron & steel market opaque and impersonal, **Ahantime** is the smart marketplace that pairs an expert AI advisor with transparent live prices and guaranteed delivery times — so you buy **right**, not just fast. Unlike the dozens of look-alike price-list sites, **Ahantime actually helps you decide.**

### Brand promise (the soul)
**«اول مشورت، بعد خرید.»** — *First advice, then purchase.*

### Vision
To become Iran's most trusted name in buying iron & steel — the first place people go not just to *check a price*, but to *make the right decision*.

### Mission
Replace the opaque, phone-only, look-alike iron market with a transparent, intelligent, genuinely helpful experience — on the web and in the app.

### Values
1. **شفافیت / Transparency** — honest, live prices; no games.
2. **دانایی / Expertise** — we know steel, and we share it freely.
3. **همراهی / Companionship** — we're on the buyer's side.
4. **سرعت / Speed** — faster answers, faster delivery.
5. **سادگی / Simplicity** — minimal, clear, effortless.

### Archetype
**The Sage** (the knowledgeable advisor — primary) + **The Companion / Everyman** (accessible, on your side) + a measured note of **The Ruler** (market authority, reliability). This trio lets one brand reassure a first-time home-builder *and* command the respect of a veteran contractor.

### Audience — serve both, no compromise
- **پیمانکار / پروژه — The Pro (B2B):** contractors, fabricators, project buyers. Want **speed and precise data.** Enter through the fast price tables; use the AI for instant lookups and پیش‌فاکتور.
- **سازندهٔ شخصی — The Builder (Retail):** individuals building or renovating. Want **guidance and confidence.** Enter through the AI advisor and the project estimator.

> The dual-mode design (AI hero **+** fast price tables) serves both without forcing either through the other.

### Competitive advantage — the «چرا آهن‌تایم؟»
1. **مشاور هوشمند، نه فقط لیست قیمت** — a smart advisor, not a price board.
2. **قیمت شفاف و لحظه‌ای** — transparent, live prices.
3. **زمان تحویل تضمینی** — guaranteed delivery time (our signature innovation).
4. **سریع‌تر و مقرون‌به‌صرفه‌تر** — faster and better value.
5. **پشتیبانی انسانیِ کاردان** — expert human follow-up (the traditional close, done warmly).

## 04 · Logo

### Concept
A minimal **I-beam (تیرآهن)** — the universal glyph of structural steel — paired with a single **amber spark**, the mark of the intelligent companion. **Steel + intelligence, in one quiet symbol.**
Files: `ahantime-symbol.svg` (app tile) · `ahantime-logo-horizontal.svg` (lockup).

### Variants
1. **Symbol / app icon** — graphite rounded tile + white I-beam + amber spark.
2. **Horizontal lockup** — symbol + wordmark (Persian primary «آهن‌تایم»; Latin «AHANTIME» for app stores / global).
3. **Wordmark only** — for tight spaces.
4. **Monochrome** — single ink or single white (stamps, invoices, embossing).

### Persian wordmark
Set **«آهن‌تایم»** in **Estedad Bold**, optical letter-spacing tightened ~2%, baseline aligned to the symbol's center. The Persian lockup is the **primary** logo for the Iranian market; Latin is secondary.

### Clear space & minimum size
- **Clear space:** a margin equal to the height of the I-beam's top flange on all sides — nothing intrudes.
- **Minimum size:** symbol 24 px (favicon / app); full horizontal lockup 120 px wide. Below that, use the symbol alone.

### Misuse — never
No stretching / skewing · no recoloring outside the palette · no shadows or bevels · no rotation · no gradient on the beam · no busy photo background without the tile · no outline versions · don't turn the spark into emoji/clip-art.

## 05 · Color

Minimal by rule: **≈70% neutral & white space · ≈20% ink · ≈10% accent** (cobalt + amber combined). One accent leads a screen; the other is a highlight only.

### Core palette
| Token | Name | HEX | Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| Ink | **Poolad Ink** | `#171C22` | Primary text, logo, dark surfaces |
| Graphite | **Graphite** | `#232A33` | Dark UI surfaces, footer, app tile |
| Slate | **Slate** | `#2B333D` | Body text on light |
| Accent | **Cobalt** | `#2E6BFF` | Smart/AI cues, links, focus, selection |
| Accent | **Cobalt Deep** | `#1F54E0` | Accessible link/text on white (AA) |
| Action | **Molten Amber** | `#F5961E` | Primary CTAs, price emphasis, the spark |
| Action | **Amber Deep** | `#C9740B` | Amber text/icons needing contrast |

### Neutrals
| Name | HEX | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Mist | `#F5F7FA` | Page background |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Cards, tables, sheets |
| Hairline | `#E5E9F0` | Borders, dividers, table grid |
| Muted | `#64707E` | Secondary text, captions |

### Functional — DATA ONLY (never decorative)
| Name | HEX | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Gain | `#15A34A` | Price up / مثبت نوسان, ticker up |
| Loss | `#E0322B` | Price down / منفی نوسان, ticker down |

### Rules
- **Cobalt** is the "intelligence" signal — reserve it for AI, interactive, and focus states.
- **Amber** is precious — one primary action per view, plus price emphasis. Overuse kills it.
- **Green/red** appear **only** inside price/ticker data — never as UI accents.
- WCAG AA: body text Slate `#2B333D` on Mist/White; links on white use **Cobalt Deep**; never set body text in plain Cobalt or Amber.

## 06 · Typography

### Families — all open-source (zero licensing cost, web-safe)
- **Persian — Primary (headings & UI):** **Estedad** (variable). Modern, geometric, premium — the brand voice in type.
- **Persian — Data & numerals (tables):** **Vazirmatn** with **tabular (monospaced) figures** so price columns align perfectly.
- **Latin:** **Inter** — the Latin wordmark, app-store text, any English.
- **Numerals:** Persian digits (۰۱۲۳۴۵۶۷۸۹) in the Iranian UI; always tabular inside price tables.

### Type scale (px / line-height)
| Token | Size / LH | Weight | Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| Display | 40 / 48 | 800 | Hero headline |
| H1 | 32 / 40 | 700 | Page titles |
| H2 | 24 / 32 | 700 | Section titles |
| H3 | 20 / 28 | 600 | Card / subsection |
| Body-L | 18 / 30 | 400 | Lead paragraphs |
| Body | 16 / 28 | 400 | Default text |
| Small | 14 / 22 | 400/500 | Labels, captions |
| Caption | 12 / 18 | 500 | Meta, timestamps |

### Rules
- One headline weight per view; let size and space create hierarchy, not many weights.
- **RTL-first:** all layouts, alignment, and icon mirroring assume right-to-left.
- Table numbers: tabular, aligned within their column, with a clear «نوسان» color + arrow.

## 07 · Iconography & Imagery

### Icons
- **Custom line set:** 1.75 px stroke, rounded joints, 24 px grid, single-color (Ink or Cobalt), drawn as one family.
- **Product-category icons** (میلگرد، تیرآهن، پروفیل، ورق گرم، ورق سرد، نبشی/ناودانی، لوله) are core assets — they power the fixed right-side category rail that turns **name → image on hover**. Equal visual weight, one perspective.

### Photography
Elevated, high-contrast, **minimal:** clean shots of steel sections and construction with cool, controlled light and lots of negative space. No cluttered stock collages, no cheesy handshakes, no clip-art. Fewer elements, more space.

### AI presence
Represented by the **amber spark** motif + **Cobalt** accent — restrained, never a cartoon mascot. The AI should read as a *capable expert*, not a toy.

## 08 · Design Principles (minimal & premium)

1. **Space is a feature.** Generous margins and padding; let content breathe.
2. **One accent at a time.** Cobalt *or* Amber leads a view — never both shouting.
3. **Data is the hero.** Price tables: clean grid, tabular numerals, clear نوسان, last-updated timestamp, «تحویل تضمینی» badge.
4. **Calm motion.** Subtle (150–250 ms ease), purposeful — the ticker scroll, the hover name→image flip, streaming AI text. No bounce, no spin.
5. **Trust is designed.** Show eNamad / اتحادیه / mill logos, real address & phone, last-updated time, and «قیمت ما vs قیمت پایه» where possible. Honesty as a visual.
6. **Mobile-first / RTL-first.** Most traffic is mobile; build the PWA accordingly — it also smooths the path to the native app.
7. **Remove before you add.** If an element doesn't move the funnel (**Magnet → Engage → Capture → Convert → Retain**), cut it.

## 09 · Core UI Components (brand-defined)

- **AI Search Hero** — a single, central rectangle, the page's focal point: «سلام، من آهن‌تایمم. چه محصولی می‌خواید بخرید؟» Cobalt focus ring, suggested-question chips beneath, streaming answers. **Unmistakably an AI.**
- **نبض بازار ticker** — a slim ribbon above the hero: dollar / euro / gold (Iran) / global ounce / steel billet (admin-entered), each with Gain/Loss color + arrow.
- **Category rail (fixed, right side)** — names that flip to category icon/image on hover; click → price table.
- **Price table** — columns: استاندارد · وزن · قیمت · نوسان · تاریخ · **زمان تحویل** · actions (علاقه‌مندی · چارت · اکسل · تصویر با لوگو · پرینت). Last-updated stamp always visible.
- **پیش‌فاکتور card** — generated from any row or AI quote; logo header; one amber CTA «ثبت درخواست».
- **Club popup** — minimal, **intent-timed** (after a quote or alert), inviting to «باشگاه آهن‌تایم».
