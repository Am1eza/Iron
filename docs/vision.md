# Ahantime — Product Vision
## Layer 1 · Vision — North Star

**Version:** 1.0 · 26 June 2026
**Companion docs:** `docs/market-research.md` (evidence), `brand/brand-book.md` (identity).
**Purpose:** The single north-star that every later decision traces back to.

---

## The Problem
Iran's iron & steel market is opaque, impersonal, and look-alike. Hundreds of near-identical sites publish قیمت روز, but none of them **help a buyer decide**, none make pricing **transparent**, and almost none let you **complete a confident purchase**. Buyers — from نervous first-time home-builders to busy contractors — are left calling around, distrusting markups, and guessing quantities.

## Vision Statement
> **To become Iran's most trusted place to buy iron & steel** — the first destination people go not just to *check a price*, but to *make the right decision*.

## Mission
Replace the opaque, phone-only, copy-paste price market with a **transparent, intelligent, genuinely helpful** experience — web, web app, and mobile app — that pairs a grounded **AI advisor** with honest **live prices**, a signature **guaranteed delivery time**, and a modern, human-closed sales flow.

## Brand Idea & Promise
- **Brand:** **آهن‌تایم / Ahantime** — "steely / made of steel."
- **Essence:** همراه هوشمند، خرید مطمئن — *a smart companion, a confident purchase.*
- **Promise:** **«اول مشورت، بعد خرید.»** — *First advice, then purchase.*

## Positioning
For Iranian builders, contractors, and buyers who find the iron market confusing and impersonal, **Ahantime is the smart steel marketplace** that combines an expert AI advisor with transparent live prices and guaranteed delivery times — so you buy **right**, not just fast. *Unlike the dozens of look-alike price-list sites, Ahantime actually helps you decide.*

## Who We Serve (priority)
- **Co-primary:** the **Contractor (پیمانکار)** and the **Individual Builder (سازندهٔ شخصی)** — served by **dual-mode** (fast tables vs guided AI).
- **Secondary:** Trader, Industrial buyer, Engineer (influencer), and the Casual Price-Checker (funnel top).
- *(Full analysis: `product/user-prioritization.md`.)*

## Strategic Pillars (how we win)
1. **Trust through transparency** — honest live prices, last-updated stamps, bourse-vs-our-price.
2. **A grounded AI advisor** — consults before quoting; never invents a number.
3. **Guaranteed delivery time** — our ownable differentiator.
4. **Modern but traditional sales** — lead-gen + پیش‌فاکتور + warm human close (no online payment yet).
5. **A premium, unmistakably-crafted experience** — the "Engineered Calm" design system; never looks templated.
6. **Mobile-first, Persian-native** — Cafe Bazaar/Myket reach; RTL/Jalali/Toman done right.

## What Makes Us Different (from the research)
- **Vs Iranian incumbents** (cluttered, red, phone-only price lists): calm, transparent, AI-guided, real conversion.
- **Vs the failed "Amazon-for-steel" plays abroad** (Reibus/Felux/XOM): we don't bet on a thin neutral marketplace; we lead with **trust + advice + a real sell-side relationship + credit/logistics later** — the model that actually works in markets like ours (China/India).

## Success Metrics (north-star KPIs)
- **Magnet:** returning price-checkers; session duration.
- **Engage:** AI conversations completed; tools used; tables viewed.
- **Capture:** leads created, alerts set, club sign-ups.
- **Convert (offline):** lead → sales-qualified → won.
- **Operate:** % SKUs fresh daily; time-to-publish prices.
- **Brand:** "the trusted, modern one."

## Guiding Principles
1. **Funnel, not brochure** (Magnet → Engage → Capture → Convert → Retain).
2. **Grounded AI** — code decides numbers; the model talks.
3. **Trust is a feature**, never traded away.
4. **Admin is the spine** — manual prices, fast & fresh.
5. **Premium minimalism** — opinionated, not templated.
6. **No dead-ends** — every state moves the user forward.

## Scope Boundaries (now)
- **In:** AI advisor, live price tables, ticker, lead/پیش‌فاکتور/CRM, alerts, club, content, tools, trust, admin.
- **Out (for now):** online payment, two-sided marketplace, auctions, native apps (Phase 3), autonomous AI publishing.
